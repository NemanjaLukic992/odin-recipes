lasagna

voila lasagna is ready

Ingridients

  • lasagna noodles
  • italian sausage
  • ground beef
  • onion, diced
  • garlic cloves
  • tomatoes
  • tomate paste
  • water

Steps

  1. spread about a cup of meat sauce into 9x13 pan. Add a layer of noodles
  2. Top the noodles with some of the cheese mixture
  3. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles nad sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Remove foil, top with mozzarella nad parmesan and bake another 15 minutes
